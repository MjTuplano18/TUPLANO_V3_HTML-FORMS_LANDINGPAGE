Our History

Mj Tuplano, the innovative founder of Step Brew, revolutionized the café scene with their creation of the iconic iced coffee. With a deep-rooted passion for coffee and a keen eye for flavor, Mj meticulously crafted a recipe that captured the essence of chill refreshment in every sip. Their dedication to quality and innovation set Step Brew apart, drawing in a loyal following of coffee enthusiasts eager to experience Mj's culinary masterpiece.

From its debut, Mj's iced coffee became the talk of the town, with patrons flocking to Step Brew to indulge in its harmonious blend of rich coffee and invigorating chill. The café buzzed with excitement as customers savored each glass, savoring the unique blend of flavors that only Mj could deliver. As demand surged, Step Brew's reputation as a purveyor of exceptional iced coffee solidified, cementing Mj's status as a trailblazer in the world of specialty beverages.

But beyond its delicious taste, Mj's iced coffee became a symbol of community and connection, drawing people together in shared appreciation of a simple pleasure done exquisitely well. Whether enjoyed alone as a moment of personal indulgence or shared amongst friends on a warm summer's day, it served as a reminder of the joy found in the simple pleasures of life. Today, as Step Brew continues to flourish, Mj's iced coffee remains a cornerstone of the café's identity, a testament to the enduring legacy of a founder whose passion and creativity continue to inspire.
